Features & Commands

Discover the full suite of modules available within Count Check, engineered to optimize OSRS progression, combat tracking, and financial portfolios. Each category is accompanied by its respective slash commands.

1. Quantitative Sizing & Portfolio Commands

These commands formulate tax-adjusted recommendations and manage your simulated capital balances.

/flip
Parameters:horizon, diversified|Required:No, No

Generates recommendations using Risk-Weighted Sorting (A/B grades first). Choices: default, short (Fast), or long (Overnight).

/f2p
Parameters:diversified|Required:No

Generates risk-weighted recommendations tailored strictly to Free-to-Play bounds.

/budget
Parameters:amount|Required:Yes

Configures your capital limit profile (supports shorthand like 10m, 1.5b).

/bot

Launches the unified, stateful interactive dashboard. Offloaded off-thread to prevent gateway lags.

/botbudget
Parameters:amount|Required:Yes

Adjusts the background paper-trading simulator's balance cap.

/help

Displays a menu listing your active price watchers with an options dropdown to delete them.

/debug

Flat public modal allowing users to securely submit bug reports or suggestions.

2. Market Intelligence & Arbitrage Commands

These commands scan G.E. margins, decanting loops, and alchemy margins.

/analyze
Parameters:item_name|Required:Yes

Compiles a 3-page dossier on price-channels, reversion risks, and undercut timelines. Retains icon thumbnail across all page clicks.

/compare
Parameters:item_a, item_b|Required:Yes, Yes

Side-by-side metric comparison contrasting margins, ROI, and relative correction risks.

/potion

Scans for profitable Bob Barter decanting loops based on active buy limits. Displays clear step-by-step instructions.

/sets

Scans for packing/unpacking set arbitrage loops compared to completed boxed sets.

/alch

Displays the top 10 tax-free High Alchemy conversions based on dynamic nature rune costs.

/breakouts

Scans the last 3 days of market activity to detect meaningful volume breakouts while filtering out illiquid noise. Results highlight items trading at multiples of their usual volume (e.g. x3.2 Surge).

/graph
Parameters:item_name, timeframe|Required:Yes, Yes

Renders a price path chart plotted against a highly optimized low-opacity volume area-fill. Timeframes: day, week, month, year.

/track
Parameters:item_name, price, direction|Required:Yes, Yes, Yes

Configures a price watcher alert.

/watchlist

Displays a menu listing your active price watchers with an options dropdown to delete them.

3. Game Mechanics & Drop Simulators

These commands simulate combat drops, caskets, and skilling pet rates.

/kill
Parameters:monster, quantity, debug|Required:Yes, Yes, No

Simulates drops for combat bosses. Sized up to 10,000 kills. Outputs full-width, un-embedded files with an aspect ratio minimum of 6 rows.

/open
Parameters:container, quantity|Required:Yes, Yes

Simulates opening minigame caskets, chests, or supply crates. Outputs full-width, un-embedded files.

/clue
Parameters:type, quantity|Required:Yes, Yes

Simulates opening clue caskets (Beginner to Master). Outputs full-width, un-embedded files.

/pet
Parameters:pet_name|Required:Yes

Rolls exact drop chances continuously in memory until the target pet is unlocked.

4. Player Progression Trackers

These commands pull player stats, pet lists, and collection logs.

/temple stats
Parameters:rsn|Required:Yes

Renders classic, un-embedded, wide 840px stats panels with a 4-column boss grid.

/temple pets
Parameters:rsn|Required:Yes

Displays your unlocked pet collection grid. Highlight and embed turn green upon completion (69/69).

/temple clog
Parameters:rsn, category|Required:Yes, No

Renders categorized collection logs. Complete categories turn green; incomplete turn yellow.

/temple update
Parameters:rsn|Required:Yes

Submits a scrape request to Temple to parse your fresh Jagex highscore datapoints.

/jagex stats
Parameters:rsn, gamemode|Required:Yes, No

Parses your official Jagex Lite highscore JSON metrics into an un-embedded, wide panel.

5. Administrative & Prefix Developer Commands

These commands are prefix-based (triggered with !) and can only be executed by the bot owner in your private administrator channel. They bypass the public slash tree entirely.

!sync

Syncs the global slash command trees with Discord's gateway.

!globalsync

Alternative handler to force-sync and purge command caches.

!botreset

Safely purges all simulated transaction histories, open orders, and trade balances from the database. Resets mock capital to 10,000,000 GP.

!debugview

Queries and lists all active, unresolved tester bug reports and suggestions.

!debugresolve <id>

Deletes a completed report from the database by its unique entry ID.

!post <#channel> <include_button_bool> <Title> | <Description>

Utility to post a custom embed to any server channel with the option to attach your persistent green Verify Agreement button view.
